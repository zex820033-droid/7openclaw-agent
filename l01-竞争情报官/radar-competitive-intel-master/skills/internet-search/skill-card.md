## Description: <br>
Guides agents on using the internet_search tool with category routing, query formulation, SearXNG syntax, result counts, and multi-search strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jzakirov](https://clawhub.ai/user/jzakirov) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to decide when and how to perform web searches for current events, research papers, community opinions, and information beyond model training knowledge. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search queries may be sent to the configured SearXNG service and upstream search engines. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include passwords, API keys, private documents, or sensitive personal details in search queries. <br>
